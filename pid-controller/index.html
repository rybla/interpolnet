PID Controller Visualizer

Adjust P, I, D values to stabilize the drone at the target altitude.

How to use:

  • Proportional (Kp): Increases thrust based on distance to target. High values cause oscillation.
  • Integral (Ki): Accumulates error over time. Helps eliminate steady-state error (droop) caused by gravity.
  • Derivative (Kd): Reacts to rate of change. Dampens oscillations.

Try setting Ki and Kd to 0, then increase Kp until it oscillates. Then add Kd to stop oscillation. Finally add Ki to reach the exact target.
